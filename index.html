Iridium: When There's Nothing

"Iridium: When There's Nothing" is a Mod-Pack that based after you beat the Ender Dragon, But wait who's that guy?!?

Learn More!

Gameplay

The world has Ended! Now you, the player, must survive in this fragmented world of floating islands known as Aetheria. Scavenge for resources, explore unique biomes, and restore the world to its former glory. Your journey will be filled with challenges, but with determination and skill, you can thrive in this desolate yet beautiful landscape.

Unique Features

Iridium: When There's Nothing offers a range of unique features that set it apart from other modpacks. Discover new biomes like the Luminous Caverns and Ice Islands, each filled with rare resources and hidden secrets. Utilize powerful new items such as the Void Crystal Shard and Sky Hook to navigate and survive the treacherous floating islands. Harness the energy of the Aetherium Reactor Core to power your advanced machinery and unlock new technologies.

Biomes

Explore the diverse and mysterious biomes of Aetheria:

  • Void Plains: Barren landscapes with scarce resources, but rich in Void Crystals.
  • Luminous Caverns: Underground biomes glowing with bioluminescent plants and rare minerals.
  • Sky Lava Fields: Dangerous fields of flowing lava, home to powerful resources and rare ores.
  • Windy Highlands: High-altitude islands with strong winds, perfect for wind energy generation.
  • Ice Islands: Cold, icy biomes with unique flora and fauna adapted to the freezing temperatures.
  • Ancient Temples: Ruins of old civilizations, filled with ancient relics and hidden dangers.
  • Crystal Meadows: Beautiful fields filled with colorful crystals and magical plants.
